## Findings

### BUG: `GET /api/reservas/{id}` does NOT return `tieneBorradorE1Pendiente`

The `ObtenerReservaController.aResponse()` method at `apps/api/src/reservas/interface/obtener-reserva.controller.ts` line 67 does NOT include `tieneBorradorE1Pendiente` in the DTO mapping. The field is present in the pipeline endpoint (`GET /api/reservas`) but absent in the detail endpoint (`GET /api/reservas/{id}`).

**Impact (critical):** The `FichaConsultaPage` loads data via `GET /api/reservas/{id}`. Since the field is absent (`undefined`), `reserva.tieneBorradorE1Pendiente` evaluates to falsy and `AccionesConsulta` NEVER blocks the action buttons. Step 7 (frontend) is coded correctly in the component but never fires in production.

**Fix needed:** Add `tieneBorradorE1Pendiente` to `ObtenerReservaController.aResponse()` and to `ObtenerReservaQuery` / its adapter.

## Issues Found

### BUG-1: AccionesConsulta block never fires (critical)

`GET /api/reservas/{id}` (`ObtenerReservaController`) does not include `tieneBorradorE1Pendiente` in its response DTO. The `FichaConsultaPage` loads the reserva from this endpoint, so `reserva.tieneBorradorE1Pendiente` is always `undefined` in the ficha.

**Files to fix:**
- `apps/api/src/reservas/interface/obtener-reserva.controller.ts` — add `tieneBorradorE1Pendiente` to `aResponse()`
- `apps/api/src/reservas/application/obtener-reserva.query.ts` — add field to `ReservaDetalleLectura` if needed
- May also need the adapter to calculate or query the field from `COMUNICACION`

### BUG-2: catalogo-plantillas.spec.ts regressions (2 tests)

The `variablesRequeridas` for E1 changed from `['email']` to `['nombre', 'tipoE1']` and a new active `ca` entry was added. The existing spec (from US-045) needs to be updated to reflect this.
